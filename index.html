About Global Milers Shipping

Global Milers Shipping and its network are committed to collaborating to build a world-class express freight network. 

From express courier packages and parcels to general freight pallets and complete truckloads, to ocean and airfreight container moves, we provide the most comprehensive variety of express courier and general freight services.

From Argentina to Russia, our sites, fleet, and people span the United States. Our collaborative culture guarantees that our one-of-a-kind network provides the highest quality of service every day.

E-cert update & reminder from Global Milers Shipping: 1 December 2020

E-cert Requirements per Animal Products Notice 2018: Regulated Control Scheme It is a requirement that relevant items be readily recognizable as being intended for export when booking export qualifying animal product transactions. For export qualified dairy goods, batch numbers must also be submitted to Global Milers Shipping. If Global Milers Shipping is not informed that the freight is E-cert, it will be transported as general freight and the E-cert eligibility will be lost. Customer Requirements When booking a cargo, we must be informed that it is an E-cert shipping and given an ED number (export document number).
